03 // TRUST DISRUPTION

The Draco team couldn't break down doors, so they broke the criminals' trust. They engaged in a 2.5-year cycle of releasing free decryptors.

1. Draco releases free decryptor.

2. Criminals lose money & update code.

3. Draco breaks the new code. Repeat.

Result: Affiliates lost faith in the product and the business collapsed.
